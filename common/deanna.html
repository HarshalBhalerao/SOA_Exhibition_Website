DEANNA DAVIS

I have been working on this for 20 years. It is a brief history of cinema told through oil paintings and text of experiences from my life. If my math is right, which it almost never is, after this is finished no one will be able to see these movies without thinking about me. Hopefully, everyone that never understood what I was doing and why, now has some resolution. Telling your supportive family that you want to pursue an art career is like telling them you want to buy a ferret. Why? I believe in what I’m doing, I’ve stood my ground, proved to myself and everyone that I thought I needed to prove myself to. That this is worth it. It is. I’m very impressive. I didn’t give up even after all those times I gave up. All the people that tell you that you can’t like Adrian in Rocky 4 will change their minds after they see you achieve your goal. It’s deeper that, they don’t know. They haven’t a clue that the art world is a multi billion dollar business. To be fair, I don’t understand how they can do what they do, I’m not like I’m actively trying. I’m grateful for having a creative mind, I’m grateful for having the means to express myself. With this project coming to a close, I’m happy to move forward. May the Schwartz be with you.
