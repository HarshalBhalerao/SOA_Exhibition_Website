The Hyperlink as a Narrative is an exploration of the performative qualities embedded within digital platforms and how they are capable of being designed with the intent to influence an individual’s ability to effectively navigate the digital space. The poster series works to emulate the visual language displayed throughout the web platform through the large scale and bold visuals that work to immerse viewers within a physical space in a similar fashion to the quick, eye-catching qualities of the web. The manual however, is more-direct reference to the visual language of the website, and provides an ambiguous series of step-by-step instructions to help guide your experience in the digital space I have created.
